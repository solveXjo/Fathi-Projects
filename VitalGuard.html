Vital Guard

A healthcare technology app designed to enhance patient-doctor interactions, simplify appointment scheduling, and improve communication efficiency.

Tools Used:

  • Next.js
  • Node.js
  • MongoDB

Project Description:

Vital Guard is a secure and scalable platform allowing patients to register, view doctor availability, book appointments, and receive real-time notifications. Designed for efficiency and reliability, the app enhances healthcare access while maintaining high-security standards.
