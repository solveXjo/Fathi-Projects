Zadni App

Unlock the potential of scholarly research with our innovative app designed specifically for Arabic-speaking scholars, students, and researchers. The app improves accessibility to Arabic research content, aiding researchers in understanding academic papers more efficiently.

Tools Used:

  • Flutter
  • Firebase

Project Description:

The Zadni App is a platform designed to make Arabic research content more accessible to scholars and students. It provides tools for better understanding academic papers, including summaries, translations, and annotations. The app is built with modern web technologies to ensure a smooth and responsive user experience.
